Andy Warhol

Visit the Warhol Foundation

Andy Warhol is a designer I admire because of how bold and ahead of his time he was. His work didn't just make a statement it changed the way people saw both art and design.Andy Warhol was bold and ahead of his time. As a Pop Art pioneer, he transformed everyday items into thought-provoking works using color, repetition, and mass media. His blend of commercial and fine art still influences ads, album covers, and branding today.


Jackson Wang

Jackson Wang spotify

Jackson Wang is an incredibly talented artist I’ve admired since his GOT7 days. Originally from China, he pursued music in Korea and became fluent in multiple languages through sheer determination. Now a successful solo artist, he’s known not just for his music, but for his vibrant personality, creative direction, and authentic self-expression.


Naoko Takeuchi

Check out more info about Naoko Takeuchi

Naoko Takeuchi, the creator of Sailor Moon, built a magical world that felt real and relatable. Her strong, stylish characters left a lasting impact on fans and designers alike. She blended fantasy with emotion in a way that still inspires me today Sailor Moon was one of the most memorable romances I watched growing up.
